# Camber Co LTD - Legal and Admin Setup

_Don't overthink this. Most of it takes a day._

---

## Company Registration

- Register Camber Co LTD on Companies House (£12, takes 24 hours)
- Sole director: Charlie
- Register with HMRC for Corporation Tax
- Decide on financial year end

## Contractor Agreement for Joe

- Written agreement covering: scope of marketing work, payment terms, IP ownership, confidentiality, notice period
- Make sure IP clause is clear: anything Joe creates for Camber Co belongs to Camber Co
- IR35 considerations: ensure the arrangement genuinely looks like a contractor relationship (Joe controls his own hours, uses his own equipment, can work for others)
- Template is fine for this, no need for a solicitor unless you want one

## Business Bank Account

- Starling Business or Tide are fast to set up
- Keep business money completely separate from personal accounts
- Set up a simple bookkeeping tool (FreeAgent, Xero, or QuickBooks)

## Insurance

- Professional Indemnity Insurance: required for consulting. Covers you if advice causes a client financial loss. Budget £300-600/year
- Public Liability: needed if you visit client premises
- Cyber Insurance: worth considering given your domain

## Contracts and Documents

- Client engagement contract template (scope, payment terms, liability, IP ownership)
- NDA template (clients will ask for this)
- Statement of Work template for individual projects
- Simple proposal template

## Tax Considerations

- VAT registration: mandatory once revenue exceeds £90,000/year, voluntary before that
- Dividend vs salary split for tax efficiency
- Set aside 25% of profits for Corporation Tax from day one
- Get an accountant. Don't DIY your taxes as a Ltd.
