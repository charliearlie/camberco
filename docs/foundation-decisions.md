# Camber Co LTD - Foundation Decisions

_You're running this solo. These decisions are yours to make, which means they get made fast._

---

## Structure

- Sole director and owner: Charlie
- Joe hired as a contractor for marketing
- No equity split, no partnership agreement, no shared decision-making complications

## Joe's Contractor Arrangement

- Define scope: what exactly is Joe responsible for? (LinkedIn content, lead generation, brand, outreach?)
- Day rate or monthly retainer? Agree terms in writing.
- Contractor agreement covering: scope, payment terms, IP ownership, notice period
- Keep it clean: Joe invoices Camber Co, you pay him. No ambiguity.

## Revenue and Pay

- All revenue belongs to Camber Co
- Charlie decides when to take dividends vs reinvest
- Joe gets paid his contractor rate regardless of business performance
- Set a personal runway target: how many months of expenses do you want in the business account before you go full-time?

## Decision Making

- It's your call. Every time. This is the upside of going solo.
- Use Joe as a sounding board for marketing decisions but final say is yours.

## Time Commitment

- How many hours per week are you committing to Camber Co alongside your day job?
- What's your target date for going full-time?
- How many hours per week/month are you buying from Joe?
