## Core Services

### AI Audit (Lead Service)

- First 5-10 audits delivered by Charlie
- All revenue from early audits goes back into the business for reinvestment
- Offer audits to ALL types of companies, not just tech firms

### Sole Traders and Tradespeople

- Set up AI-powered bots (OpenClaw or similar) to handle:
  - Scheduling and diary management
  - Invoicing and payment chasing
  - Parts ordering and inventory
  - Customer enquiries and quoting
- This is a massively underserved market. Plumbers, electricians, builders are drowning in admin and have zero tech support. Low price point but high volume potential.

### AI Content Generation Training

- Show businesses how to generate marketing content, social posts, blog articles, email campaigns using AI
- Specific training on Claude Code for content workflows
- Not just "here's ChatGPT", but structured workflows that produce consistent, brand-appropriate output

### Reducing Consultancy Costs

- Show businesses how to use AI to handle legal research, compliance checking, policy drafting, and legislation interpretation
- NOT replacing lawyers, but reducing the hours spent (and billed) on routine legal/compliance work
- HR policy generation, contract review assistance, regulatory compliance checklists

### AI-Powered Software Development

- Show businesses how coding agents (Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot) can build features faster and cheaper
- Help software engineers transition from code-writing to product engineering
- Position: "Your dev team doesn't need to be bigger, it needs to be augmented"

### Fractional CTO Service

- Charlie stays embedded in the client's business
- Ongoing role guiding the business into AI adoption
- Strategic AI roadmap, tool selection, team training, vendor evaluation
- Higher price point, longer engagement, recurring revenue
- Capacity constraint: can only take 1-2 fractional CTO clients at a time

## Additional High-Value Offerings

### AI Workflow Automation (n8n, Make, Zapier)

- Automate repetitive business processes: lead qualification, onboarding sequences, data entry, report generation, customer follow-ups
- Concrete deliverable: "We replaced 15 hours/week of manual admin with an automated workflow"
- Sell the outcome (time saved, errors eliminated), not the technology

### AI-Powered Customer Service Setup

- Build and deploy AI chatbots for websites, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger
- Handle FAQs, booking, basic support queries 24/7
- Warm handoff to humans for complex issues
- Measurable ROI: "reduced support response time from 4 hours to 4 minutes"

### Internal Knowledge Base / AI Assistant

- Companies have knowledge trapped in people's heads, shared drives, and Slack threads
- Build an internal AI assistant trained on their own documentation, processes, and FAQs
- New starter onboarding drops from 2 weeks to 2 days
- Killer offering for companies with 20-100 employees

### Data and Reporting Automation

- Many businesses still have someone manually pulling reports from spreadsheets
- Build AI-powered dashboards and automated reporting
- Connect to their existing tools (CRMs, accounting software, project management)
- Weekly/monthly reports generated automatically with AI-written summaries

### AI Readiness Assessment (Productised Service)

- Standardised assessment you can deliver at scale
- Score the business across: data maturity, process documentation, team capability, tool stack, security posture
- Deliver a report with prioritised recommendations
- This becomes your LEAD MAGNET. Charge £500-1000, and it feeds into bigger engagements

### Email and Communication Optimisation

- Set up AI-assisted email drafting, prioritisation, and response templates
- Meeting summarisation and action item extraction
- Sales email personalisation at scale
- Small win that demonstrates AI value quickly

### Document Processing and Extraction

- Many businesses (legal, accounting, property, insurance) process thousands of documents manually
- AI-powered extraction: invoices, contracts, applications, forms
- Huge time savings, easy to demonstrate ROI
- Particularly strong for: estate agents, solicitors, accountants, recruitment firms

### AI Security and Governance Consulting

- As businesses adopt AI, they need policies for: data handling, acceptable use, output verification, bias checking
- Most companies have ZERO AI governance
- Offer: AI usage policy, data classification framework, risk assessment
- Positions Camber Co as the responsible, trustworthy consultancy

### Competitor and Market Intelligence

- Set up AI-powered monitoring of competitors, industry news, regulatory changes
- Automated briefings delivered daily/weekly
- Valuable for sales teams, leadership, and strategy roles

### AI Training Workshops (Scalable Revenue)

- Half-day or full-day workshops for teams
- "How to use AI effectively in your role" tailored by department
- Sales teams, marketing teams, ops teams, finance teams all need different training
- Can charge £1500-3000 per workshop for 10-20 people
- Scales better than 1-to-1 consulting
