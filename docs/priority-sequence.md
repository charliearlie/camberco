# Camber Co LTD - Priority Sequence

_Do these in order. Don't skip ahead._

---

## Week 1: Set Up The Business

- Register Camber Co LTD on Companies House
- Open a business bank account
- Agree contractor terms with Joe (scope, rate, start date)
- Get a contractor agreement signed

## Week 2: Define The Offer

- Answer: what specific problem do you solve, for who, and how?
- Write a one-paragraph pitch you could send in a LinkedIn DM
- Price your services. Research competitors. Don't undercharge.
- Write up 2-3 case studies from your past work
- Brief Joe on the positioning so his marketing is aligned

## Week 3: Build Proof

- Get Professional Indemnity Insurance
- Create client contract and proposal templates
- Build 3-4 before/after demos for prospect meetings
- Joe starts LinkedIn content and outreach

## Week 4: Get Your First Client

- Run the warm network audit
- Identify 10-15 potential prospects
- Start conversations. Coffee meetings. Free audits.
- Goal: 1 paying engagement within 30 days

## What Can Wait Until Month 2+

- Website and landing page
- Logo and branding
- Business cards
- Formalised processes and templates
- Find and appoint an accountant

## What Can Wait Until Month 3+

- Paid marketing
- Hiring additional subcontractors
- Building internal tools
- Scaling beyond word-of-mouth
